Mental Health in Tech
Introduction

This dashboard contains data originally sourced via Kaggle and aims to analyse mental health in tech companies. The data visualisations below intend to give a brief overview of respondent data before analysing some of the trends contained within the data.

Gender Breakdown

Almost 75% of respondents identified as Male, compared to just under 25% for female.

Country Breakdown

Over 50% of respondents were from the US, with the UK next.

Treatment

Split between those having requested treatment and not is almost 50/50.
